1.Description

Welcome to the help page of this application!
If you are here is probably because you have questions or issues using this application, so here is a piece of help...

This application is for Log In / Log Out / Sign Up and you can see your account data after log in
As you can see at the bottom of this page, there are 3 main windows plus this help page and different pop up alerts. First of all, you need an account, for that you can make a new account in the sign in window and next(When you already have an account) log in the server.

2.Log in Window

When you launch the application, this is the first window that you'll always see.

Here you can see a log in window with two empty text fields, one called Username and the other called Password.
If you already have an account, you can log in directly filling the two text fields and clicking the LogIn button, this will check if the data is correct, if is not correct the application tells you what's going wrong. If not, you can make a new account clicking in the word here in the sentence under the texts field, this link will send you to the sign up window that we'll explain later.

3.Sign in Window

In this window you may create a new account filling the text fields with your data. Different text fields and requirements:
-Username: 4-10 characters
-Password: 8-14 characters and at least one uppercase and one number
-Repeat Password: equal to password
-Email: pattern of email(e.g. email.example@extension.cope)
-Full Name: 1-45 characters

When you finish filling the text fields, if you click on the SignUp button and the application will check your data, if there is something wrong it will be shown to you.

4.Main Window

This is the 'main' window of the application. Here you can see the data of your account plus some extra data like:
-The date of your last connection.
-The date of the last time you change your password.

As you can see, there are two bars in the window, the upper one is for options like close the application or the access to this help page, and the other one, the status bar with data about your connection, here you can see your user and the date of your last connection plus a LogOut text which is clickable and it will returns you to the LogIn window closing your connection with the server.
