1. How works the LogIn Window

When you launch the application, this is the first window that you'll always see.

Here you can see a log in window with two empty text fields, one called Username and the other called Password.
If you already have an account, you can log in directly filling the two text fields and clicking the LogIn button, this will check if the data is correct, if is not correct the application tells you what's going wrong. If not, you can make a new account clicking in the words Click here in the sentence under the texts field, this link will send you to the sign up window that we'll explain later.
