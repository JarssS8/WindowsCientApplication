1.Shortcuts

The sign up window have some shortcuts to help you

To register directly press Alt + S at the same time.

To go back to login press Alt + B at the same time.

To open help window press F1 in any window of the app.

2.Validations

Some validations you need to know

Username: 4 to 10 characters.

Email: Possibility example "example@extension.cope".

Full name: 1 to 45 characters.

Password: 8 to 14 characters.

Must have at least one uppercase and one number.
